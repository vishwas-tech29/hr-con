Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our HR consulting services

How quickly can AAMCS help with our HR challenges?

We typically begin seeing improvements within 30-60 days, depending on the scope of work. For urgent matters, we offer rapid response services with initial recommendations within 48 hours.

Do you work with small businesses and startups?

Absolutely! We specialize in helping small businesses and startups establish strong HR foundations. Our scalable solutions are designed to grow with your business.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have extensive experience across IT/tech, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, and professional services. Our team stays current with industry-specific HR challenges and regulations.

How do we get started with AAMCS?

It's simple! Contact us for a free 30-minute consultation. We'll discuss your needs and propose a customized approach to address your HR challenges.

What's the cost of your HR consulting services?

Our pricing is tailored to each client's specific needs. We offer flexible engagement models including project-based, retainer, and hourly options to fit your budget.

Can you help with compliance for Indian labor laws?

Yes, our team has deep expertise in Indian labor laws including the Shops and Establishments Act, Industrial Disputes Act, PF, ESI, and other statutory requirements. We help ensure full compliance.
